Biography

I am a first-year PhD student in the Chinese University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. Currently, I am working with Prof. Justin Solomon as a visiting Ph.D student in the Geometric Data Processing Group at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Prior to joining CUHK, I obtained my bachelor degree in Statistics from Chongqing University. My research mainly focuses on optimization theory and designing provably efficient optimization algorithms for machine learning.

I am actively looking for postdoc/research positions in optimization and machine learning.
