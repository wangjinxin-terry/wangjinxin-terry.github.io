Biography

I am Jinxin Wang, a third-year PhD student at the Chinese University of Hong Kong advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So.

Prior to joining CUHK, I obtained MPhil in Data Science (2017-2020) from Peking University supervised by Prof. Zaiwen Wen, and bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics (2013-2017; Loo-Keng Hua Talent Program) from Dalian University of Technology. In addition, I was an exchange student (2016-2017) under the supervision of Prof. Xin Liu at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS), the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), and an intern in Didi Chuxing (2019; Mentor: Dr. Zhe Xu) as well as Huawei Theory Lab (2019-2020; Mentor: Mr. Gong Zhang and Dr. Fan Zhang).

My research interest lies in optimization theory and algorithm design in data science and signal processing, especially in nonconvex statistical estimation (distance geometry), matrix optimization, and optimization under uncertainty.

Papers

[7] Randomized Submanifold Subgradient Method for Weakly Convex Optimization over Stiefel Manifold.
Andy Yat-Ming Cheung, Jinxin Wang, Man-Chung Yue, Anthony Man-Cho So.
TL;DR. BCD for problems with nonlinear coupling constraints; BCD for optimization problems with a single Stiefel manifold constraint (instead of product of manifolds); coordinate descent without coordinate.

[6] Heteroscedastic Probabilistic PCA via Generalized Power Method.
Jinxin Wang, Chonghe Jiang, Huikang Liu, Anthony Man-Cho So.
TL;DR. Estimation performance of GPM at a linear rate for HQPOC using error bound results; non-convex statistical estimation.

[5] Decentralized Non-Smooth Optimization Over the Stiefel Manifold. [arxiv]
Jinxin Wang, Jiang Hu, Shixiang Chen, Zengde Deng, Anthony Man-Cho So.
TL;DR. Convergence guarantees of decentralized algorithms for coupled non-smooth non-convex optimization using proximal smoothness.

[4] Projected Tensor Power Method for Hypergraph Community Recovery.
Jinxin Wang, Yuen-Man Pun, Xiaolu Wang, Peng Wang, Anthony Man-Cho So.
Proceedings of the 40th International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML 2023).
TL;DR. Convergence guarantee of PTPM for a non-convex polynomial optimization problem; non-convex statistical estimation; low-rank structure.

[3] Orthogonal Group Synchronization with Incomplete Measurements: Error Bounds and Linear Convergence of the Generalized Power Method. [arxiv; in preparation for Mathematical Programming]
Linglingzhi Zhu, Jinxin Wang, Anthony Man-Cho So.
TL;DR. Localization over the orthogonal group; spherical Euclidean distance matrix; non-convex statistical estimation; low-rank structure.

[2] Sparse High-Order Portfolios via Proximal DCA and SCA. [paper]
Jinxin Wang, Zengde Deng, Taoli Zheng, Anthony Man-Cho So.
Proceedings of the 2021 IEEE International Conference on Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing (ICASSP 2021).
TL;DR. Proximal DCA and SCA for a non-smooth difference-of-convex optimization problem.

[1] Joint Bandwidth Allocation and Path Selection in WANs with Path Cardinality Constraints. [paper]
Jinxin Wang, Fan Zhang, Zhonglin Xie, Zaiwen Wen, Gong Zhang.
Journal of Communications and Information Networks (JCIN), 2021.
TL;DR. Built on the classic network utility maximization, we consider a more practical problem with multiple zero-norm constraints and tackle it using ADMM.
