Biography

I am a postdoctoral principal researcher working with Prof. Bahar Taşkesen at the University of Chicago Booth School of Business. I am passionate about optimal transport and optimization.

I received a PhD degree in Operations Research at CUHK in Feb 2025 fortunately advised by Prof. Anthony Man-Cho So. I also get valuable advice from Prof. Huifu Xu and Prof. Viet Anh Nguyen.

I obtained an MPhil degree in Data Science (2017-2020) from Peking University fortunately supervised by Prof. Zaiwen Wen, and a bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics (2013-2017; Loo-Keng Hua Elite Program; minor in Finance) from Dalian University of Technology fortunately advised by Prof. Liwei Zhang. In addition, I was an exchange student (2016-2017) under the great supervision of Prof. Xin Liu at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science (AMSS), the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS).
